# Biomarker Reference Ranges

Clinical reference ranges for all supported biomarkers. Sources: AHA/ACC, ADA, AACE, KDIGO, Endocrine Society guidelines.

## Lipids

| Marker            | Unit  | Normal  | Optimal | Critical | Direction        |
| ----------------- | ----- | ------- | ------- | -------- | ---------------- |
| Total Cholesterol | mg/dL | 125-200 | <200    | >240     | Lower is better  |
| LDL-C             | mg/dL | 0-100   | <100    | >160     | Lower is better  |
| HDL               | mg/dL | 40-100  | >60     | <40      | Higher is better |
| Triglycerides     | mg/dL | 0-150   | <100    | >500     | Lower is better  |
| ApoB              | mg/dL | 0-90    | <80     | >120     | Lower is better  |

## Metabolic

| Fasting Glucose | mg/dL  | 65-100  | 65-90   | >126     | Lower is better |
| Fasting Insulin | uIU/mL | 2-25    | <6      | >25      | Lower is better |
| HOMA-IR         | —      | 0-1.0   | <1.0    | >2.5     | Lower is better |
| Uric Acid       | mg/dL  | 2.5-7.0 | <5.0    | >9.0     | Lower is better |
